Design Philosophy

To us garganutan humans a quantum dot may not seem like a giant, but among the world of atoms, a quantum dot is magnificent in size. The MEM·C design transforms its visitor's perspective to that of an atom's with a subtly bold design. The spherical shapes of atoms and quantum dots suggests circular patterns that make frequent appearance throught the design.

A unique consequence of a quantum dot's geometry is a phenomenon known as quantum confinement, in which a a quantum dot's diameter determines the color of the light it emits: violet for the smaller dots and red for the larger dots. This effect is captured in the color transitions when hovering over interactive elements; as headers descend in size their colors transition from red phosphorous, to yellow sulfur, green copper, blue cobalt, and violet manganese.

At least that's how I imagine Apple would sell it.
